This project was made for a small non-profit organization that focuses on lifestyle to improve their way of raising and managing charitable funds. The main goal was to create a fundraising portal based on blockchain technology that would make everything transparent, trustworthy, and efficient throughout the whole fundraising process.

Thanks to the smart contract created, the fundraising process has been improved, providing transparency at every stage of donated money lifecycle. Donors can now track how funds are being used in real-time, resulting in maximum clarity and trust in the nonprofit's activities.

Moreover, the use of blockchain technology has simplified the process of receiving funds, by removing intermediaries and streamlining the financial management process. Every transaction is securely recorded on the blockchain, ensuring complete transparency and facilitating the demonstration of appropriate use of funds.

As a result, the fundraising portal not only provides transparency and trust but also enhances the operational efficiency of the non-profit organization, enabling it to focus more on its mission to make a positive contribution to the community.

Through this work, we offered clear and detailed guidance to the startup, enabling them to tackle the challenges and opportunities presented by their ambitious initiative. We helped them integrate the latest Web3 technologies and understand the benefits of NFT technology.
